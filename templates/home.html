What is Formula One and why is it so great?

Formula 1, or F1, is a globally celebrated motorsport featuring cutting-edge cars, elite drivers, and a rich history. Known for its high-speed races on circuits worldwide, F1 showcases technological innovation, team dynamics, and thrilling drama. The sport's international appeal, environmental efforts, and cultural impact make it a beloved spectacle that continues to captivate millions of fans, combining speed, strategy, and human excellence on a global stage.
